Module outgoing

These are functions that your plugin may need in order to work with LDH.

It is mentioned which functions are required for Libraries, Dependents, and both, so don't worry.

Functions

LDHDisable () Informs your plugin of LDH disabling.
LDHVerify () Allows LDH to verify your plugin is registered properly.
LDHLibOn (plugin_name) Notify your plugin of a library plugin "turning on".
LDHLibOff (plugin_name) Notify your plugin of a library plugin "turning off".
