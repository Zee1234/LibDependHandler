Module incoming

Functions within LDH that your plugin will call, generally relating to registration.

Some only apply to Dependents, some only apply to Libraries. However, one plugin can be both a Dependent and a Library, so choose functions to use accordingly.

Functions

RegisterDependent (a_plugin, ...) Register your plugin as a Dependent.
UnregisterDependent (a_plugin) Unregister your plugin as a Dependent.
AddLibs (a_plugin, ...) Add required Libraries to your list (Dependent only).
RemLibs (a_plugin, ...) Remove required Libraries to your list (Dependent Only).
RegisterLibrary (a_plugin) Register your plugin as a Library.
UnregisterLibrary (a_plugin) Register your plugin as a Library.
